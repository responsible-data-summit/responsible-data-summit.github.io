Responsible Data Technology and Policy in the Real World

Technologists and businesses are innovating on the development and use of new privacy technologies that are making the promise of big data a reality. This track shares learnings and experiences from top academics, corporate leaders and others about new privacy computing technologies and how it’s used in real world applications. Meanwhile, with GDPR, CCPA, and other federal laws under debate, regulations and policies to both protect consumers from data breaches and expedient uses of their data have come to the forefront. Hear from top regulators and consumer advocates on their approach to privacy legislation, trade offs, and the future of public policy and responsible data use.
